## Blog Post Specifications

Every blog post must include:

- **Word count:** 1,500–2,500 words
- **H1:** Contains primary keyword naturally
- **H2s:** 4–6 subheadings with secondary keywords
- **Internal links:** Min 3 (to Products, Services, Pricing, Contact, or other blog posts)
- **CTA:** WhatsApp quote button + phone number at bottom
- **FAQ section:** 3–5 questions with `FAQPage` JSON-LD schema
- **Meta title:** Under 60 characters, keyword-first
- **Meta description:** 150–160 characters, includes CTA
- **Image:** Min 1 relevant image with descriptive alt text

## Internal Linking Strategy

Every blog post should link to:

1. **Product pages** — When mentioning specific roofing profiles (long span → `/products`, stone-coated → `/products/stone-coated`)
2. **Pricing page** — When discussing costs (anchor: "check our current price list" → `/pricing`)
3. **Services page** — When mentioning installation (anchor: "our installation services" → `/services`)
4. **Contact/Quote** — Every post ends with CTA (anchor: "get a free quote" → `/contact`)
5. **Related blog posts** — Link to 2–3 related posts in a "Related Reading" section at bottom
6. **Location pages** — When mentioning specific states (anchor: "delivery to [State]" → `/locations/[state]`)
